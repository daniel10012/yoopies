Explore Endpoints


/GET METHOD
  • All Salaries
  • Specific Commune Salary (ex for GEOCODE 75101)
  • Specific Commune Geo Shape (ex for GEOCODE 75101)
  • All coordinates of communes
/POST METHOD
  • Post a new salary for a commune
    • body has to contain a JSON including the GEOCODE (zip), LIBGEO (commune name), SNHM14 (average salary) and geo_point_2d (coordinates)
/PUT METHOD
  • Update the salary info for a specific commune
    • body has to contain a JSON of the changing fields. CODGEO and LIBGEO cannot be changed
/DELETE METHOD
  • Delete the entire salary info for a specific commune
